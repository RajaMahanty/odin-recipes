Veg Pulao

Description

Veg Pulao is a simple yet delicious Indian rice dish made by cooking rice with mixed vegetables and aromatic spices. It's perfect for lunch, dinner, or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cloves
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • ½ cup chopped carrots
  • ½ cup green peas
  • ½ cup chopped beans
  • 1 green chili (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Wash and soak rice for 20 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pot. Add bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, and cinnamon.
  3. Add onions and sauté until golden. Add tomatoes and cook till soft.
  4. Add chopped vegetables, green chili, and sauté for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Add soaked rice and sauté for a minute. Pour in water and add salt.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 15–20 minutes until rice is done.
  7. Fluff with a fork, garnish with coriander, and serve hot.
