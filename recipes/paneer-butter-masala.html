Paneer Butter Masala

Description

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich and creamy Indian curry made with soft paneer cubes simmered in a mildly spiced tomato-based gravy. It's a popular vegetarian dish served with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp cream
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil and 1 tbsp butter in a pan. Add onions and sauté until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Blend the mixture to a smooth paste and return to the pan.
  4. Add chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, sugar, and salt. Cook for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Add paneer cubes and cook for another 5 minutes on low heat.
  6. Stir in cream and the remaining butter. Simmer for 2 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
